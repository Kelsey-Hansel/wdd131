Choose Your Adventure Game

A game where you can choose the adventure you have and help make the game better! Optional domain availability: choose-your-adventure-game.com

Purpose:

We provide you a customizable character and the start to your adventure and you provide your imagination and preferences, and any feedback you have.

Scenarios:

  • What do I want my character to be?
  • What choices should I make on my adventure?
  • How do I submit feedback about my experience?

Color Schema:

  • Primary Color - rgb(4, 37, 4) - used on header and footer as background color.
  • Secondary Color - rgb(102, 168, 102) - used on main as the background color.
  • Accent Color 1 - rgb(8, 1, 48) - used as text color in main.
  • Accent Color 2 - rgb(255, 255, 255) - used as text color in header and footer.

Typography:

Advent Pro - Will be used in different weights for the whole document.
